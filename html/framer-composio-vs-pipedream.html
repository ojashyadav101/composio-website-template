Pipedream taxes your user growth.

Pipedream charges for identity, not activity. You pay ~$2/month for every user who connects an account—even if they never use your agent again. This destroys your unit economics, especially for freemium or high-growth products where users might be dormant.

Lucy charges only for results.

We offer Unlimited Authenticated Users on all plans. You only pay when your Agent actually performs an action (like creating a Jira ticket). Your costs align perfectly with the value you deliver, ensuring you never pay a "tax" just because a user signed up.
